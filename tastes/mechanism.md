# Taste: mechanism

Foreground how a thing works — the causal account, the update rule, the pathway from
cause to effect. When you read a source, pull out the mechanism it proposes and the
evidence that the mechanism (not just the outcome) is real. Ask what the moving parts
are and how they interact. Surface tensions about *why* something works, especially
where two works agree on an outcome but disagree on the mechanism behind it.

This shapes emphasis only — not page structure, support requirements, or evidence
discipline.
