# Taste: construct

Foreground what is being defined and measured — the construct, its operationalization,
and whether the measure captures the concept it claims to. When you read a source, pull
out how it defines its key terms and what it actually measured as a stand-in for them.
Ask whether the operational definition matches the conceptual one. Surface tensions
where two works use the same word for different constructs, or measure the same
construct in non-comparable ways.

This shapes emphasis only — not page structure, support requirements, or evidence
discipline.
